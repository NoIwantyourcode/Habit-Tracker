# Habit Tracker

Do you have any habits that you may want to leave... Or do you have any habits you may want to gain, Well now with habit tracker you can now track your daily activities and habits! 

Be empowered to leave the habits you want to leave and gain new, better habits that you want!

# Features
here are all the features of the website!

1. Add/delete habits
2. Daily check-in (mark complete) - Press this button to mark your habit as done for the day 
3. Streak tracking - Tracks for many days you do your habits in a row
4. Best streak - the longest amount of time you have done a habit without stopping
5. Completion rate (30 days) - tells you how much of your habit you have done over 30 days
6. 30 day heatmap - shows how many times you did your habit over 30 days!
7. Edit habit name - made a mistake in naming your habit? Change it!
8. Dark/light mode toggle - change how the website looks at will
9. localStorage - stores all your habits locally
